technology

 

 

Smartphones: A smartphone is a mobile phone with advanced features and capabilities, such as the ability to access the internet, send and receive emails, and run various applications (apps).

Operating Systems: The operating system (OS) of a smartphone is the underlying software that controls and manages the phone's functions and features. The most popular smartphone operating systems are iOS (Apple), Android (Google), and Windows Phone (Microsoft).
